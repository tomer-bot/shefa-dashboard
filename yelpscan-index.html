What does the free scan actually check? +
We review your Yelp profile completeness, campaign configuration, response time history, budget utilization, CTR vs. category benchmarks, and review activity. You get a scored report across 5 dimensions with specific findings — not a generic checklist.
What's the difference between certified and Platinum Partner? +
Certified means you've passed Yelp's basic training. Platinum is the highest tier — it requires sustained high-volume spend and proven client performance. Platinum Partners have a direct Yelp contact, access to the Partner API, advanced analytics, and early access to beta features. It's a meaningful distinction, not a marketing label.
Does the AI bot sound robotic? +
No — we configure it to match your business's tone and personality. It introduces itself as your assistant, not as a bot. Most customers don't realize they're not talking to a person until they come in for the job. We test every bot with sample conversations before going live.

Primarily local home service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, roofing, moving, construction, cleaning, electrical. If your customers find you on Yelp and call to book a job, we can help you get more of them and make sure none go unanswered.
